Throughout my PhD, I developed a strong interest in using mathematical and computational tools to explore complex biological processes. I’m passionate about advancing this field and continuously learning new techniques to better understand the intricacies of life sciences.

Currently, I’m a postdoctoral researcher at the IBDM lab in Marseille, working under the supervision of Léo Guignard.
